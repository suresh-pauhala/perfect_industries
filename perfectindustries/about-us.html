About Us

Incepted in the year 2005 at Ahmedabad (Gujarat, India), we "Perfect Industries" are a notable and prominent Sole Proprietorship firm that is engaged in Manufacturing a wide range of Sheet Metal Press Tool, Punching Die, Press Die, Bending Dies, etc. Offered products are well-known for features like fine finish, durability and high strength. Additionally, we impart Sheet Metal Press Job Work, Tool Room Consultancy as per client's need. Under the headship of "Mr. N K Malvi" (Proprietor), we have gained a remarkable and strong position in the national market.
